Here is my writing style, so we don’t forget it...

Writing styles I base mine from:

* [Microsoft writing Style](https://learn.microsoft.com/en-us/style-guide/welcome/).
* [PracticalTypography](https://practicaltypography.com).
* APA style on
  * [Numbers & Statistics](https://owl.purdue.edu/owl/research_and_citation/apa_style/apa_formatting_and_style_guide/apa_numbers_statistics.html)
  * [Footnotes](https://owl.purdue.edu/owl/research_and_citation/apa_style/apa_formatting_and_style_guide/footnotes_and_endnotes.html).
  * [Citations](https://apastyle.apa.org/style-grammar-guidelines/citations), substituting the in-text references by less intrusive tooltips.
* <Q {...q.hyperlink}>Not putting verb phrases in hyperlinks</Q>.
